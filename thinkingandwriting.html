How writing helps in thinking...

Writing is a way to organize your thoughts and ideas in a paper so you can see what you are actually thinking.
Writing helps you gather all your thoughts in one place and is a way to give your thoughts an existing shape.
Actually, we don't take our thoughts seriously unless we write them up, and it is a roadmap to your thought
process, so you could see the reasoning behind your thoughts and exactly see why you have such perspectives on
certain things.
Most of the things don't get an appropriate structure unless you associate them with an action, thinking gets
into action when you start to write the thoughts. Writing is basically a tool for thinking.
